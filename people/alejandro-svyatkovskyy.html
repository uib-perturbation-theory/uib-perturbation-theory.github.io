Research interests

Alejandro works on black hole perturbation theory, gravitational-wave ringdown, quasinormal modes and late-time tails. His current research focuses on understanding how black hole resonances are excited and how they can be extracted from time-domain signals.

Selected topics

  • Black hole ringdown and quasinormal modes
  • Time-domain black hole perturbation theory
  • Late-time tails
  • Numerical methods for gravitational-wave physics
